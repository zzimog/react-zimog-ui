  ## Group & Addons
  ### Wrap your `<Input />` in a `<Input.Group />` component and add `<Input.Addon />` to show icons or others decorative elements in your field.

  <Card>
  <Content>
    <div className="mx-auto flex max-w-md flex-col gap-4">
      <Input.Group>
        <Input.Addon>
          <User />
        </Input.Addon>
        <Input id="demo-input-1" placeholder="Username" />
      </Input.Group>
      <Input.Group>
        <Input.Addon>http://</Input.Addon>
        <Input id="demo-input-2" placeholder="your-subdomain" />
        <Input.Addon>.example.com</Input.Addon>
      </Input.Group>
      <Input.Group>
        <Input id="demo-input-3" placeholder="name.surname" className="text-right" />
        <Input.Addon>@example.com</Input.Addon>
      </Input.Group>
    </div>
  </Content>

```tsx
<Input.Group>
  <Input.Addon>
    <User />
  </Input.Addon>
  <Input placeholder="Username" />
</Input.Group>

<Input.Group>
  <Input.Addon>http://</Input.Addon>
  <Input placeholder="your-subdomain" />
  <Input.Addon>.example.com</Input.Addon>
</Input.Group>
```

  </Card>
</Article>
